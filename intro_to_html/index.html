5 facts about Whistle Pigs

Source: National Geographic

  1. They build burrows 8-66' long, including areas to defecate (bathrooms)
  2. When they hibernate they reduce their heartrate as slow as 5 beats per minute
  3. They are loners! Most of the year they spend on their own until it's lovin' time.
  4. Even though they can be called woodchucks, they have nothing to do with wood!
  5. They are distant relatives to squirrels
